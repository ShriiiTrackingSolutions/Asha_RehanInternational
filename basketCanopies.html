What is Basket Canopies ?

Basket canopies are a type of overhead shading structure characterized by their unique design resembling a basket or woven pattern. These canopies consist of a framework of supporting elements, such as metal or wood, arranged in a basket-like pattern, onto which a canopy material is stretched or woven. The canopy material can be fabric, metal, or other suitable materials depending on the desired aesthetic and functional requirements.

Basket canopies are commonly used in outdoor spaces such as parks, gardens, patios, and commercial areas to provide shade and enhance the visual appeal of the surroundings. They offer a distinctive and stylish alternative to traditional canopy designs, adding texture and interest to the architectural landscape.
